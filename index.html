A little bit about me.

I have always had the gift of connecting to people. I am a naturally happy person. When the world shut down, I realized I had to compensate and pivot. I discovered Web Design and working remotely as many others did. I found Wed Design could be an art, connecting and communicating with others. It delights me when I can successfully help their businesses grow and develope.

Now I've made a full switch to website design and development and couldn't be happier. With my sharp, cultivated skills of organization and building rapport with individuals from so many years in Customer Service and an eye for detail I'm free to write clean, elegant code. I would never go back.
